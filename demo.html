Sample receipts — test without a real bill.

nota. splits a restaurant bill: the host scans the paper receipt, then guests open a link and claim what they had. You can test the whole flow right here — no real receipt needed. Tap a receipt below to enlarge it on this screen, then scan it with the app.

  1. Open nota. and complete the one-time setup (your name + a payment number — any number works for testing).
  2. Tap “Scan the receipt”, then tap a receipt below to enlarge it and point the camera at this screen. Good light, no glare — hold steady until it captures. Tip: you can capture several receipts in one go — tap the shutter for each, then Save, and they merge into one bill.
  3. The items appear as editable rows. Tap “Create session” — a QR code and a link (app.notan.nu/j/…) are generated.
  4. Open that link in any browser (this device or another) to see the guest side: tap items, see your share, and a payment button appears.

If scanning off a screen is tricky, the app also lets you enter a bill manually (always free) — same result. Receipt photos are read on-device; nothing is uploaded except the text of the items.
