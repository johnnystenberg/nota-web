How it works

Four taps from receipt to paid.

1

Scan the receipt

Snap the paper receipt — the text is read on your device and split into tappable rows. Or type it in by hand.

2

Show the QR code

The table scans it with their camera — or you send the link by SMS. No app, no sign-up.

3

Everyone taps what they had

Guests pick their dishes in the browser. Shared a bottle of wine? Both tap it and the price splits to the öre.

4

They pay you directly

One tap opens Swish, Vipps or MobilePay with the exact amount. The money goes straight to you — the app never touches it.
